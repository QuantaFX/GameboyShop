About Us

Our Story

Gameboy Cartridge Co. was founded by a group of passionate retro gaming enthusiasts who grew up in the golden age of handheld gaming. We remember the excitement of unwrapping a new Game Boy cartridge, the satisfying click as it locked into place, and the countless hours of adventure that followed.

Our mission is to preserve these memories and share them with new generations of gamers. Every cartridge in our collection has been carefully sourced, authenticated, and tested to ensure it delivers the same magical experience we remember from our childhood.

What We Do

We specialize in authentic Game Boy cartridges from the original Game Boy era through the Game Boy Advance. Our inventory includes:

  • Original Game Boy cartridges in excellent condition
  • Game Boy Color exclusive titles and compatible games
  • Game Boy Advance cartridges from launch to the final releases
  • Rare and limited edition releases
  • Complete-in-box sets with original packaging

Every item undergoes a rigorous inspection and cleaning process. We test each cartridge multiple times on authentic hardware to guarantee functionality. Save batteries are tested and replaced when necessary to ensure your game progress is preserved.

Our Commitment to Quality

We stand behind every product we sell with a comprehensive quality guarantee:

  • All cartridges are authentic Nintendo products
  • Each item is cleaned, tested, and verified before shipping
  • Save batteries are replaced on games requiring them
  • We provide detailed condition descriptions and photos
  • 30-day return policy on all purchases
  • Secure packaging to ensure safe delivery

Join Our Community

Gameboy Cartridge Co. is more than just a shop. We are building a community of collectors, players, and enthusiasts who share a love for classic handheld gaming. Connect with us on social media to share your collection, discuss favorite games, and stay updated on new arrivals and exclusive offers.

Whether you are rebuilding your childhood collection, discovering these games for the first time, or searching for that one elusive title, we are here to help you on your retro gaming journey.
