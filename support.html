Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders typically arrive within 5-7 business days. International shipping times vary by location, usually 10-21 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Products must be in the same condition as received. Contact us to initiate a return.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. The funds should appear in your account within 7-10 business days depending on your bank.

Product Information

Are all cartridges authentic?

Yes, we only sell authentic Nintendo cartridges. Each item is thoroughly inspected and verified before listing.

Do the save batteries work?

We test all save batteries and replace them when necessary. If a cartridge requires a save battery, we ensure it is fresh and functional.

What condition are the cartridges in?

Each listing includes a detailed condition description. Most of our inventory ranges from good to excellent condition with minor cosmetic wear consistent with age.
