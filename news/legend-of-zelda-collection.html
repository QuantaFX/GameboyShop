Legend of Zelda Returns to Our Collection

October 5, 2025
Collection Update

It is dangerous to go alone, so take this! We are thrilled to announce that we have secured a limited quantity of authentic Legend of Zelda cartridges for the original Game Boy. This iconic adventure title has been a cornerstone of handheld gaming since its release, and now you have the chance to own a piece of gaming history.

The Legend Lives On

The Legend of Zelda for Game Boy brought Link's adventures to the palm of your hand for the first time in 1993 with Link's Awakening. This groundbreaking title proved that epic adventures were not confined to home consoles, delivering a complete Zelda experience on Nintendo's portable powerhouse.

Each cartridge in our collection has been carefully authenticated using our rigorous verification process. We examine every detail, from the label artwork to the circuit board construction, ensuring that what you receive is a genuine Nintendo product from the golden age of gaming.

Condition and Authentication

Our Legend of Zelda cartridges are in excellent condition, with clean labels and fully functional internals. Every unit has undergone extensive testing on authentic Game Boy hardware to verify:

  • Complete game functionality from start to finish
  • Properly working save battery with fresh replacement
  • Clean and corrosion-free contacts
  • Authentic Nintendo circuit board and components
  • Original label in good to excellent condition

Save Your Adventure

One of the most critical aspects of any RPG cartridge is the save battery. All of our Legend of Zelda cartridges have been fitted with brand new CR2025 batteries, ensuring your progress through Koholint Island will be preserved for years to come. We test each save battery multiple times to guarantee reliability.

Limited Availability

Due to the rarity and collectible nature of authentic Legend of Zelda cartridges, our supply is extremely limited. These cartridges have been sourced from trusted collectors and verified for authenticity. Once this batch is sold, it may be months before we can secure additional copies.

We recommend acting quickly if you want to add this legendary title to your collection. Each purchase includes our 30-day guarantee and lifetime authentication support.

Relive the Magic

Whether you are rebuilding your childhood collection, experiencing this classic for the first time, or adding to your Game Boy library, now is the perfect time to secure your copy of The Legend of Zelda. Explore dungeons, solve puzzles, battle enemies, and uncover the mysteries of one of gaming's most beloved franchises.

Visit our shop today to view available copies and pricing. Adventure awaits!
